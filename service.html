Project 365

Project 365 by Chi Psi Beta is the Philanthropy of the fraternity I am part of. The goal of Project 365 is to raise mental health awareness and shed some light on a serious problem that is often times overshadowed. This event supports the cause through Charity Dinners and Silent Walks. The Silent Walk is a half-day event where brothers walk around campus in silence to show our support. And the Charity Dinner/ Date Auction is an event where supporters can bid on a date to contribute to our philanthropy. This cause is very deeply embedded into the core value of Chi Psi Beta because we lost a fellow brother to a mental health-related disease.
